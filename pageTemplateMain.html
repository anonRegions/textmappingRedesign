Why Use Scrolls?

Why use scrolls? Because scrolls are better than books! They are much better for teaching and learning. No other book form — ancient or modern, paper or digital — can match the unrolled scroll for these important, teacher/learner–friendly attributes:

  • clarity and transparency
  • ease of navigation
  • random access
  • multisensory access
  • rich, comprehensive support for...
    • exploration and discovery
    • skimming, scanning, deep reading and comprehension
    • strategic, big–picture thinking
    • the use of concrete thinking to drive abstraction
    • shared learning and engaged discussion
    • instruction that is literally explicit — i.e., unrolled (ex + plicare), hence wide open to understanding. This is the original, ancient meaning of the word — a word coined by scribes to describe the rolled books of antiquity.
